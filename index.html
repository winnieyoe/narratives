The Framework

Thank you for agreeing to take part in this exericse. There are 4 parts to it — you will first read an article, then complete 4 simple tasks based on the article. This is not a test, there are no right or wrong answers. The whole process should take about 15 minutes. Your participation is anonoymous — I will not be collecting your name, contact information, or other identifiers.

Part 1: Highlight Three Sentences

Please read through the article. Click to select 3 sentences you think are the most important. Successfully selected sentences will be highlighted in yellow. To change your selection, click on the sentence again to deselect it. Submit once you have decided the 3 sentences.


Part 2: Type Three Words

Please type in three words in response to the article you just read. It can be a thought/feeling you have, or a keyword from the article.





Part 3: Select One Image

Please select the image you think would be best suited as the article's headline image.
